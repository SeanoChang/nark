The Najdorf Sicilian arises after 1.e4 c5 2.Nf3 d6 3.d4 cxd4 4.Nxd4 Nf6 5.Nc3 a6.
The move 5...a6 prepares ...e5 or ...b5 without allowing Nb5. It is favoured by
attacking players including Fischer and Kasparov.
