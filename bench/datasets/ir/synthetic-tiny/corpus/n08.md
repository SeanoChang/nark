I2C bus capacitance is typically 100–400 pF. For 100 kHz Standard mode, a
4.7kΩ pull-up gives a rise time well within spec; at 400 kHz Fast mode you
usually want 2.2kΩ. Calculate Rp,max = tr / (0.8473 × Cb).
